🤖 About The Project

AI Powered Student Dropout Prediction System

Project Goal

Predict whether a student is likely to Graduate, remain Enrolled, or Dropout using Machine Learning algorithms and academic indicators.

Artificial Intelligence

The model analyzes age, scholarship, semester grades, tuition status, and academic performance to generate accurate predictions.

Prediction Results

Predict Graduate, Enrolled, or Dropout status along with confidence scores to support early intervention.
